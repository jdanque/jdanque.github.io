TreeView for Trello

A tree style view for Trello

Have you ever tried to organize a tens of lists with tens of cards? Tired of scrolling just to count how many cards are in your lists? TreeView can help you out. TreeView is a Trello powerup that displays your board, lists and cards in a tree style view giving users an overview of their board. Users can see how many cards are in a list without scrolling. Collapse those other lists to focus on your current list. Users can also drag and drop lists and cards. Default theme of TreeView is gray but you can easily change the theme to another one to complement your board. You can even hide labels and badges for a more minimalized view.
